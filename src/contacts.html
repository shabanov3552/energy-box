Adapter / Labour

For vehicles with bottom battery mounting, Tsapa Adapter is available at $15 per set.

Service station labour charges is around $20 for most cars where batteries are accessible (i.e.. engine bay, trunk)

Battery Terminals

There are two sizes of terminals predominant in car batteries DIN & JIS.

Tsapa battery includes DIN terminal but service stations are equipped with both terminals.
